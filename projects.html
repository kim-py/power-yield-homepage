Project Criteria

What We Look For
in a Project

Project Stage

Shovel-ready or late-stage development projects with all key permits in place and a clear path to construction.

Technology

Onshore wind, offshore wind, utility-scale solar PV, or hybrid projects with proven technology and established supply chains.

Geography

European markets preferred; Middle East and select international markets considered on a case-by-case basis.

Project Size

Minimum € 20M total project volume. Larger projects with co-investment structures are especially welcome.

Developer Track Record

Established developers with at least one completed project preferred. First-time developers may be considered with strong partnerships in place.

Revenue Visibility

PPAs, feed-in tariffs, Contracts for Difference, or other contracted revenues strongly preferred. Merchant exposure should be clearly quantified.
