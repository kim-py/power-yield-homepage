Power Yield was built by a team with deep roots in infrastructure finance, renewable energy development, and digital platforms. We saw firsthand how institutional investors access top-tier energy projects — and how private investors are consistently left out of the equation, not because of a lack of interest, but because of structural barriers that have never been challenged at scale.

Our platform applies institutional investment logic — threshold-based funding, milestone reporting, project-level structuring — to make fractional infrastructure investing accessible, transparent, and credible. We believe the quality of a deal should be determined by the merits of the project, not the minimum ticket size.

We are currently in the pre-launch phase, building our investor waitlist and finalising our first project partnerships across Europe. We work with established developers, independent energy assessors, and legal advisers to ensure that every project on our platform meets the standards we hold ourselves to.

If you share our vision — whether as an investor, a developer, or a potential partner — we'd love to hear from you.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Transparency First

Every project on our platform comes with full documentation, clear timelines, and regular milestone updates. We share the risks as clearly as we share the opportunity. No hidden fees, no opaque structures.

Institutional Standards

We apply the same rigour as top-tier infrastructure funds to every project we select and structure. Independent technical assessments, legal review, and financial modelling are non-negotiable for every listing.

Measurable Impact

Clean energy output and CO₂ savings are not marketing language — they are tracked, reported, and verifiable. Every project includes an ESG impact report updated with each milestone.
