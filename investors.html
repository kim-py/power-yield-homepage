Why Power Yield

Fractional Access

Invest in projects normally reserved for family offices and institutions, starting with smaller ticket sizes. Power Yield removes the minimum investment barriers that have historically excluded private investors from infrastructure.

Threshold-Based Safety

Your capital is only deployed when a project reaches its full funding target. No partial deployments, no half-built projects. If a project does not reach threshold, your commitment is returned in full.

Milestone Transparency

Follow your investment through every phase with structured reporting on construction, commissioning, and operations. We apply the same reporting standards used by institutional infrastructure funds.

Understanding the Risks

Infrastructure investments are illiquid and long-term in nature. Returns are projected based on energy yield models, PPA contracts, and market assumptions. All financial figures presented on this site are targets or estimates — not guarantees.

Specific risks include construction delays, variability in energy production, changes in energy pricing, regulatory developments, and currency exposure. Each project will be accompanied by a full risk disclosure document.

Capital at risk. Power Yield does not provide regulated investment advice. Please consult a qualified financial adviser before committing capital. Past performance of comparable assets is not indicative of future results.
